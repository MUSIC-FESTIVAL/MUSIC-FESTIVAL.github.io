Welcome

Get ready to immerse yourself in a world of vibrant music, energetic performances, and unforgettable experiences. This annual event, held at the Estadio Alejandro Serrano Aguilera in the picturesque city of Cuenca, Ecuador, has been a celebration of rock music for several years.

Event Details

The "Unleash the Sound" rock festival has been an annual event in Cuenca, Ecuador, for several years. This year, it has taken place at the Estadio Alejandro Serrano Aguilera, offering a range of ticket options to suit different preferences. General admission tickets have ranged from $20 to $100, with VIP tickets available for those who want a more exclusive experience. The festival has offered a lineup of diverse bands, including iconic groups like The Beatles, Queen, Led Zeppelin, and The Rolling Stones. The headline act has been the legendary rock band Queen, who have delivered an electrifying performance that has left audiences in awe. Overall, the festival has been a celebration of music and art, bringing together performers and audiences from around the world in a vibrant and energetic atmosphere.
